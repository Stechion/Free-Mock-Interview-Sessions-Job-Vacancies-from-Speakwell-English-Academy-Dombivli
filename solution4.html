IMPROVE COMMUNICATION

1.Enroll in Communication Workshops

A candidate enrolls in a communication skills workshop or seminar specifically designed for professionals. These workshops often cover topics such as effective speaking, presentation skills, and how to explain technical concepts to a non-technical audience. Participants engage in activities such as role-playing and mock presentations.

2.Practice with Speech or Communication Coaches

The candidate works with a speech or communication coach who provides personalized guidance and feedback. The coach helps the candidate practice answering common interview questions, focusing on improving articulation, tone, and body language. The sessions may include recording and reviewing practice responses.

3.Join Public Speaking Clubs

The candidate joins a public speaking club, such as Toastmasters, where they regularly practice speaking in front of an audience. The club provides opportunities to give speeches, receive feedback, and improve presentation skills in a supportive environment.Through regular practice, they become more comfortable with speaking clearly and engagingly, which translates to better performance in interviews.

4.Engage in Mock Interviews

The candidate participates in mock interviews with a focus on improving communication skills. The mock interviews simulate real interview conditions and include a focus on how well the candidate explains accounting concepts, answers behavioral questions, and interacts with the interviewer. they can work on simplifying their explanations for future interviews

5.Record and Review Practice Sessions

The candidate records themselves answering interview questions and then reviews the recordings to assess their communication skills. They focus on aspects such as clarity, conciseness, and the effectiveness of their explanations.Reviewing recordings allows the candidate to self-assess and identify areas for improvement.
